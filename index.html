About Keshthana Infrastructure


Keshthana Infrastructure private Limited is a fast growing, professionally driven organization that delivers world class Engineering, Procurement and Construction services to its clients, thereby making itself a "niche" in the Construction industry.


We commit to projects that in turn lead to national progress, not compromising on safety, quality, customer satisfaction and environmental protection that are integral part of KIPL's vision and mission.


KIPL’s reputation and businesses rely on timely completion of projects with the support of our valuable clients, consultants, sub-contractors and vendors.


We are also strongly committed towards quality policy and objectives.
